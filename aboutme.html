About me

Hey there! I'm Luis, a Computer Science Engineer with more experience in Development, Quality Assurance, Technical Consulting and Project Management. I have worked with Scrum methodology and have technical knowledge in Java, C#, Python, and JavaScript.

My experience includes developing automation tests for web applications with tools like Selenium, Appium, and Cypress. I also have experience in conducting integration, API, and performance tests with tools such as Postman and JMeter.

I am currently expanding my skillset by learning about DevOps. As a versatile engineer, I believe it's important to have a well-rounded understanding of the entire software development life cycle, from design to deployment and maintenance. DevOps, with its focus on collaboration and automation between development and operations teams, is an exciting area that has captured my interest.

In addition to my technical skills, I also have a passion for gaming, watching the best series on TV/online streaming, and playing the guitar. When I'm not working, you can usually find me exploring new worlds in my favorite video games, binge-watching the latest series on my watchlist, or strumming away on my guitar.

Fun fact: I played in the Overwatch World Cup 2023 as part of the Ecuador national team
